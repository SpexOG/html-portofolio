The Best Movies According to Sebastian

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Straight Outta Compton

I enjoyed ''Straight Outta Compton'' for its authentic portrayal and capturing of the revolutionary energy of the Hip-Hop Culture in the '80s and '90s.
I love the Hip-Hop Culture.

Wolf Creek

The suspenseful atmosphere and gritty realism kept me on the edge of my seat throughout, making it a standout in the horror genre.

Fast and Furious

Overall, "Fast and Furious" offers a thrilling escape into a world of fast cars, daring feats, and unexpected twists.
